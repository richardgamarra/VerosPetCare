📅 Bookings & CancellationsReservas y Cancelaciones

For one-time services, we recommend at least 48–72 hours in advance. For ongoing services (weekly walks, regular sitting), ideally 1–2 weeks. Holiday periods (Thanksgiving, Christmas, July 4th) fill up fast — book at least 2–3 weeks ahead for those dates.Para servicios únicos, recomendamos al menos 48–72 horas de anticipación. Para servicios continuos (paseos semanales, cuidado regular), idealmente 1–2 semanas. Los períodos festivos se llenan rápido — reserva con al menos 2–3 semanas de anticipación para esas fechas.
Cancellations made 48+ hours before the service receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24–48 hours receive a 50% refund or credit toward a future visit. Same-day cancellations are non-refundable. Emergencies are handled case-by-case — always reach out and we'll work something out.Las cancelaciones realizadas con 48+ horas de anticipación reciben un reembolso completo. Las cancelaciones entre 24–48 horas reciben un reembolso del 50% o crédito para una futura visita. Las cancelaciones el mismo día no son reembolsables. Las emergencias se manejan caso por caso.
You will be contacted immediately. We keep the contact information for your preferred vet and the nearest emergency veterinary clinic on file for every client.
